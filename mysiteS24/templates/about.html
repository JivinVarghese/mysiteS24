About the University Tech Club

The University Tech Club was founded in 2010 with the mission to foster a community of tech enthusiasts on campus. Over the years, the club has grown significantly, providing numerous opportunities for members to develop their skills and pursue their passions.

Our achievements include organizing successful hackathons, hosting renowned guest speakers, and collaborating with tech companies for internships and job placements.

Our Milestones

  1. 2010 - Club founded
  2. 2012 - First annual hackathon
  3. 2015 - Partnership with local tech companies
  4. 2018 - Membership reached 500 students
  5. 2021 - Launch of the Tech Club Mentorship Program
